While browsing for some games I happened to find a really interesting project. The name is OUYA. You can find more at "website". Basically it is a fair salon console.

Why do I say fair? Take the alternatives:

  • The paystation 3 alone cost 300 euros.
  • You cannot develop on it without paying.
  • Add three games at 70 and a controler and you double the price.
For what? A game that can run on a full-HD monitor? Full-HD is old, it is not a feature. XBOX360 is no better.
And I am beginning to think that the games on those platform aren't really ones. That's another debat.

I will take the OUYA. It is cool, you can develop or buy games if you want. The DLC if I understood will not not hinder the gameplay as you will have a complete game and not some semi part you have to complete by buying the remaining.
That is what is featured at least.
